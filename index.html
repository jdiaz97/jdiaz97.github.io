Projects

Image Classifier for Camera Traps

Neural Networks - Biodiversity

Chile has 44 national parks and 41 nature reserves, almost of all them have hundreds of camera traps to monitor wildlife. I'm building a neural network to classify those pictures.

The main constraints are having a very limited dataset and very bad pictures, but it works. It includes native species.

I'm looking forward to expanding this to also use it with drones to monitor the distribuition of invasive species like beavers.
